MUSIC FEST: DRUMMERS SUMMIT 2022

Drummers Summit 2022 is a initiative developed by world class drummers to promote drumming as a career. Drummers Summit is usually held in every two years in a selected country of the world. This is to draw the attention of drummers in selected country to the core skills and standards neccessary to build a successfull career as a drummer. It provides a platform to connect with world class drummers around the world and build wider connections amongst drummers
